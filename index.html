How to Place an Order

  1. Browse Products: Use the categories on the left or search bar to find items.
  2. Add to Cart: Click "Add to Cart" on any product you like.
  3. View Cart: Click the cart icon in the header to review your items.
  4. Checkout: In the cart, click "Checkout" to proceed to payment.
  5. Complete Order: Fill in your details and confirm the order.

If you need more help, contact our support team.
